Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Ihsan ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Data Stored on Your Device

The following information is stored locally on your device and never sent to our servers:

  • Your recovery streak and progress data
  • Journal entries and personal reflections
  • Urge and trigger logs
  • App preferences (language, notification settings)
  • Mood check-in logs
  • Prayer tracking data

Data Sent to Our Servers

The following information is sent to our servers to provide app functionality:

  • An anonymous user ID (automatically generated, not linked to your identity)
  • Anonymous usage analytics (screen views, feature interactions, and session duration)
  • Community posts and replies that you choose to submit

Community Feature

When you use the community feature, your posts and replies are stored on our servers and visible to other users. All community participation is anonymous — no personal information such as your name, email, or device is attached to your posts. You may delete your own posts at any time.

Analytics & Usage Data

We collect anonymous usage analytics to improve the app experience. This includes screen views, feature interactions, and session duration. This data is tied to an anonymous user ID and cannot be used to identify you personally. We do not collect device identifiers, IP addresses, or behavioral profiles. We do not use any third-party advertising or tracking services.

Third-Party Services

Ihsan uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — our backend provider for anonymous authentication, analytics storage, and community features. Supabase processes data in accordance with their privacy policy.
  • RevenueCat — our subscription management provider. RevenueCat processes your purchase receipts and subscription status through Apple's App Store. RevenueCat does not receive your personal information.

Content Blocker

Ihsan includes an optional DNS-based content blocker that operates through a local VPN configuration on your device. All filtering happens on your device. No browsing data, DNS queries, or internet activity is sent to our servers or any third party.

Subscription & Payments

Subscription purchases are processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID. All billing is managed by Apple according to their privacy policy.

Data Deletion

You can delete all locally stored data by uninstalling the app. To request deletion of your community posts and server-side data, please contact us at ihsanappios@gmail.com and we will process your request promptly.

No Advertising

Ihsan does not display advertisements and does not share your data with advertisers or data brokers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app with an updated revision date.
